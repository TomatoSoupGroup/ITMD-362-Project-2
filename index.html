Welcome!

We are a society of philosophers and other scholars, from around the world. We are devoted to promoting international dialogue and the discussion of fundamental questions of universal concern,

Which affect all humanity, such as world peace, human rights, dialogical interrelations of diverse cultures, and preservation of the world ecology.

We believe that open and free discussion between people who believe in ensuring the growth and preservation of all people will help us create a better narrative amongst our peers.

We believe people are inherently meant to be good, and to look out for each other, and creating an environment that encourages this belief is our primary goal.

Explore our website to learn more about us and our beliefs, and sign up for our conference to recieve further information!
