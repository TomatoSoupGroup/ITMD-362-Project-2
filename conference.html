The Austria Conference

You won't want to miss the Austria Conference this year! Sign up now for more information.

This year's conference will go over heavily on the global pandemic that is occuring right now, and the effect it has had on people around the world.

People around the world have been affected financially, emotionally, and mentally due to the stress that long term quarantining has brought about.

This year's conference will go over how people from different nations, backgrounds, and financial situations can be helped, and how the approaches will have to differ from one to the next.

Such an event is a once in a lifetime occurence, and having experienced it allows us to more effectively lay out plans that can be used to effectively help people.

With such an important topic at hand, we encourage people from around the world to participate in this discussion regarding this once in a lifetime event.
